Keynote Speakers:

Mikhail Golosov and Gregory Kaplan



The Eleventh Annual Tsinghua Workshop in Macroeconomics, sponsored by the National Institute for Fiscal Studies of the Tsinghua University, will be held on June 17-19, 2019 in Beijing. The main program of the conference will last for two days, with the third day reserved for further discussion or tour. We welcome papers in all areas related to macroeconomics.
