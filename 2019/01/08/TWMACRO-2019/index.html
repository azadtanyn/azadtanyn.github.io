Keynote Speakers: TBA



The eleventh annual Tsinghua Workshop in Macroeconomics will be held on June 17 - 19, 2019 in Beijing and is sponsored by the National Institute for Fiscal Studies of Tsinghua University. We welcome papers in all areas of macroeconomics. The final topics, however, will be determined ultimately by the interests of the participants. Each paper will be given one hour presentation time so that there will be ample time for discussions. If you are interested in presenting at the conference, please submit your paper at:

http://editorialexpress.com/conference/TWMacro2018/



The deadline for submission is Friday, March 1, 2019. The conference’s webpage is:


http://www.twmacro.com



Details about 2019 conference program will updated at:
